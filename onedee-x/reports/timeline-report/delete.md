# How to Delete Clock

## Step to Delete Clock

* Go to **Reports Menu**
* Click **Timeline**

![](../../../.gitbook/assets/screenshot_4-1.png)

* Click **Clock data**

![](../../../.gitbook/assets/screenshot_1.png)

* Click icon **Trash** for delete that clock

![](../../../.gitbook/assets/screenshot_2-3.png)

* Click **Delete**

![](../../../.gitbook/assets/screenshot_3.png)
