# Plan & Billing

## How to Upgrade Plan

* Go to **Company Menu**
* Select **Plan & Billing**
* Click **Change Plan**

![](../../.gitbook/assets/app.onedee.ai_en_leave_leave-balance-laptop-with-mdpi-screen-3.png)

* Click **Upgrade Plan**

![](../../.gitbook/assets/01-plan-cloud.png)

* Select **Amount of employee**
* Click **Continue**

![](../../.gitbook/assets/00-before-apply-code.png)
